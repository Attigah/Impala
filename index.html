Our Story

Built on Belief. Driven by Purpose.

The Impala Foundation was born out of a deep-rooted belief that every individual — regardless of their circumstance — deserves access to quality education, adequate healthcare, and a thriving community.

Our name reflects the grace, agility, and resilience of the African impala: an animal that moves together as a herd, looks after its own, and adapts to its environment. These are the values that drive our work every day.

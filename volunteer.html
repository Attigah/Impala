Why Volunteer With the Impala Foundation?

Volunteering with us is more than giving time — it is a transformative experience that connects you with communities, builds your professional and personal skills, and contributes to real, measurable change.

Whether you are a student, a professional, a retiree, or simply someone who cares — there is a role for you. Our volunteers work across education, healthcare outreaches, community development, event support, photography, logistics, and more.

You don't need to be an expert. You need heart, commitment, and a willingness to serve.

✅
Meaningful impact — your work directly benefits lives in need
✅
Skills development — leadership, fieldwork, communication & more
✅
Certificate of service — recognised volunteer experience
✅
Community & network — connect with like-minded people across Ghana
✅
Flexible commitment — one-time events or ongoing involvement

Before You Apply

What to Expect as a Volunteer

We believe in being transparent so you can make the best decision for yourself.

01

Orientation & Briefing

All new volunteers attend a brief orientation session — in-person or virtual — to understand the Foundation's values, current programmes, and code of conduct.

02

Role Assignment

Based on your skills, availability, and interest, you'll be matched to a suitable volunteer role — from fieldwork to admin, photography, health outreach, or teaching support.

03

Field or Remote Work

Some roles involve travelling to communities and programme sites. Others can be done remotely. We communicate all requirements clearly before you commit.

04

Supervision & Support

You won't be left alone. Each volunteer is supervised by a team lead and supported throughout their engagement. We are always reachable via WhatsApp and phone.

05

Flexible Duration

You can volunteer for a single event, a month, or longer. We accommodate all schedules and always appreciate whatever time you can give.

06

Certificate & Recognition

All volunteers who complete their assignment receive an official Impala Foundation Certificate of Volunteering, which can be used for CVs and professional portfolios.

Opportunities

Volunteer Roles Available

📚

Education Support Volunteer

Assist with tutoring sessions, literacy clubs, mentorship programmes, and educational material distribution at partner institutions.

🏥

Health Outreach Volunteer

Support free health camp logistics, patient registration, awareness campaigns, and health worker coordination in communities.

📸

Photography & Media Volunteer

Capture field moments, document programme activities, and create visual content for our social media and reports.

🏘️

Community Development Volunteer

Help with borehole project logistics, vocational training sessions, community outreach events, and cooperative support activities.

💻

Digital & Admin Volunteer

Support our team remotely with social media, data entry, graphic design, report writing, or fundraising communications.

🎤

Events & Fundraising Volunteer

Help plan, coordinate and run Foundation events — from community outreaches to donor engagements and fundraising drives.

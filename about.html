About the Impala Foundation

A movement rooted in compassion, driven by purpose, and committed to lasting change.

Our Story

Born Out of Belief. Built for Communities.

The Impala Foundation was born out of a deep-rooted belief that every individual, regardless of their circumstance, deserves access to quality education, adequate healthcare, and a thriving community.

Founded by a group of passionate change-makers who witnessed first-hand the challenges facing underserved communities, the Foundation set out to build a bridge between need and opportunity. What began as a grassroots effort to support children in accessing schooling has grown into a multi-programme organisation touching thousands of lives.

Our name — Impala — reflects the grace, agility, and resilience of the African impala: an animal that moves together as a herd, looks after its own, and adapts to its environment. These are the very values that drive our work every single day.

We are not just an organisation; we are a movement — one that believes in the power of community to lift itself when given the right tools and support.

Mission & Vision

Our Work

Three Pillars of Lasting Change

The Impala Foundation operates across three core pillars: Education, Community Development, and Healthcare. Together, these form a holistic approach to human development that addresses root causes — not just symptoms.

📚 Educational Development

Scholarships, mentorship, literacy clubs, and learning infrastructure for over 100 tertiary students.

🏘️ Community Development

Vocational training, borehole construction, livelihoods support, and cooperative development in Vume and Dodze, Volta Region.

🏥 Healthcare

Free health outreach camps, screenings, maternal health support, and health awareness campaigns in underserved communities.
